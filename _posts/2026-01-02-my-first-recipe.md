## Ingredients
- 2 Chinese sausages
- 2 leeks
- 1 tablespoon oil
- Salt to taste

## Instructions
1. Slice the Chinese sausages thinly.
2. Chop the leeks.
3. Heat oil in a pan, saut√© the sausages until fragrant.
4. Add leeks, cook until tender.
5. Season with salt and serve.

## Notes
Perfect for stuffing buns or dumplings.
